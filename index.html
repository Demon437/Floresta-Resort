About Us

Welcome to Floresta Farm & Resort, a serene escape newly opened for travelers seeking peace, comfort, and a closer connection with nature. Surrounded by lush greenery, fresh air, and breathtaking views, Floresta is more than just a resort—it’s an experience of harmony and rejuvenation.

Here, modern luxury meets the charm of the countryside. From cozy stays and farm-to-table dining to outdoor activities and relaxing spaces, every corner of Floresta is designed to refresh your soul. Whether you’re planning a family holiday, a romantic getaway, or a corporate retreat, we offer the perfect blend of relaxation and recreation.
